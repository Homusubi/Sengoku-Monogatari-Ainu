Note: There is no real distinction between the early and modern name categories for the Ainu. I honestly do not know whether Ainu changed their naming system much over the centuries leading up to the invasion of Aynu Mosir. Actual modern Ainu tend to have Japanese-language names (such as Kayano Shigeru), but as Civ represents completely independent civilisations, it does not make sense to use actual modern Ainu names.

Male: Onibishi, Isonash, Keutoranke, Resak, Unayanke, Shiratekka, Itakshir, Pasekur, Petennuk, Munkeke

Modern Male: Ainurakkur, Atoi, Ikasmatuk, Itaktonkor, Unakeshi, Utarian, Ekoriaci, Eraragur, Operikun, Otaushi

Female: Monashir, Etunrachich, Toitoi, Umakashte, Katkemat, Pukusa, Huisak, Umoshmatek, Opere, Unkatuye

Modern Female: Ashirirera, Apenanka, Ianpanu, Ukaechu, Ennekkon, Eshiriya, Harukor, Hucimaci, Tekatte, Maktasan
